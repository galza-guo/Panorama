# Settings

Customize Panorama to fit your needs.

## General

- **Base Currency**: This is the currency used for your global "Net Worth".
  Changing this will convert all your historical data to the new currency using
  historical exchange rates.
- **Formatting**: Choose your preferred date format (DD/MM/YYYY or MM/DD/YYYY)
  and number format (1,000.00 or 1.000,00).

## Appearance

- **Theme**: Toggle between **Light**, **Dark**, or **System** mode.
- **Privacy Mode**: Blur values on the dashboard (useful when using Panorama in
  public).

## Data Management

- **Delete All Data**: **Warning**: This will permanently wipe your local
  database. There is no undo.
