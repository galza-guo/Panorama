# Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Panorama free?

Yes! Panorama is completely free and open-source software. You can download it,
use it, and even modify it without paying a cent.

### Is my data safe?

Absolutely. Since Panorama is a local-first application, your data never leaves
your computer. It is stored in a local database that only you have access to.

### Can I import data from Wealthfolio?

Since Panorama is a fork of Wealthfolio, the database structure is largely
compatible. You should be able to import your backup files, but we recommend
double-checking your data after import.

### Does it have a mobile app?

Currently, Panorama is focused 100% on the **Desktop** experience (macOS,
Windows, Linux). We dedicate all our resources to making the best desktop
investment tracker possible.

### How do I update Panorama?

Panorama will check for updates automatically on startup. When a new version is
available on GitHub, you'll be notified to download and install it.

### What makes Panorama different from Wealthfolio?

Panorama extends Wealthfolio with specialized features for Asian markets:

- **China A-Share Support**: Native tracking for Shanghai and Shenzhen stocks
- **Chinese Fund Data**: Support for 公募基金 with data from 东方财富 and 天天基金
- **Hong Kong MPF**: Dedicated MPF tracking with fund allocation views
- **Insurance Tracking**: Comprehensive policy management

### Where is my data stored?

- **macOS**: `~/Library/Application Support/Panorama/`
- **Windows**: `C:\Users\<User>\AppData\Roaming\Panorama\`
- **Linux**: `~/.config/panorama/`
