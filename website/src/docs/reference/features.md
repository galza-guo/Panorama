# Features

Panorama comes packed with powerful tools to help you manage your wealth, with
special enhancements for Asian markets.

## Dashboard

Your financial command center.

- **Net Worth**: Real-time calculation of your total wealth.
- **Performance**: Visual charts showing your portfolio's growth over time.
- **Allocation**: Breakdown of your assets by type, sector, or custom tags.

## Activity Tracking

Log every move your money makes.

- **Transactions**: Buy, Sell, Deposit, Withdraw, Dividend, Interest.
- **CSV Import**: Easily import history from your broker.
- **History**: Searchable list of all past activities.

## Goals & Limits

Stay on track.

- **Financial Goals**: Set target net worth or savings goals and track your
  progress.
- **Contribution Limits**: Monitor your contributions to tax-advantaged accounts
  (like IRAs or ISAs) to avoid penalties.

## Market Data

- **Global Support**: Track assets from major exchanges worldwide (US, EU, Asia).
- **Crypto**: Integrated support for major cryptocurrencies.
- **Forex**: Automatic currency conversion for multi-currency portfolios.
